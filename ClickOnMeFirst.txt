Terminal aesthetic with green text on black background
Weather data including:

Temperature & feels-like temp
Humidity & cloud cover
Wind speed (km/h and knots), direction, and gusts
Atmospheric pressure
Precipitation
Weather conditions


Flight tracking with all previously requested data

Interactive commands:

Type refresh to update immediately
